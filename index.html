How to play!

  • Click button to earn money
  • Buy upgrades to increase your income
  • Autobots earn you money each second
  • Pointers increases money you earn each click
  • TicTac's reduces your ticklength by 1 milisecond
  • Tier upgrades unlocks the next tier of each upgrade
  • A tier 2 pointer (Pointerator) will give you a new Pointer for each 10 clicks
  • A tickcycle will give you Autobots and TicTacs, the tickcycle is displayed as a loading bar at the foot of the page, when it finishes it restarts
